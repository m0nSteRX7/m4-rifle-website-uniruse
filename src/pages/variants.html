Variants of the M4 Rifle

M4A1

The M4A1 is a fully automatic variant of the basic M4 carbine intended for special operations use. It has a heavier barrel to withstand the heat generated during sustained fire.

M4 MWS

The M4 Modular Weapon System (MWS) is a variant that allows for the attachment of various accessories such as optics, grenade launchers, and laser sights, making it highly versatile for different combat scenarios.

M4 Commando

The M4 Commando is a shorter version of the M4 carbine, designed for close-quarters combat. It features a shorter barrel and collapsible stock for improved maneuverability.
