History of the M4 Rifle

The M4 carbine is a 5.56×45mm NATO, air-cooled, gas-operated, magazine-fed carbine that is widely used by the U.S. military and other armed forces around the world.

Specifications

Feature Details
Caliber 5.56×45mm NATO
Weight 6.4 lbs (2.9 kg)
Length 33 inches (840 mm)
Rate of Fire 700-950 rounds/min

Notable Features

  1. Lightweight design
  2. Modular construction
  3. Versatile accessory options
  4. Effective range of 500 meters
